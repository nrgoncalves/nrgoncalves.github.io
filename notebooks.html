  • A normalization model of multisensory integration

    14 Nov 2016. Many neurons in the brain are driven by more than one sensory system (e.g. vision and audition), and they might play an important role in improving our perception of what surrounds us. Here I explore recent work by Ohshiro and colleages on the role of divisive normalization in multisensory integration.
